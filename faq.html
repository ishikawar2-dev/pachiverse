NFTs & Reveal Machine assignment, reveal timing, NFT categories 04 questions

No. Machine NFTs use a deterministic assignment architecture. Each NFT is pre-assigned before purchase — not randomly generated at the moment of reveal.

This design prevents post-launch allocation manipulation and ensures that reveal outcomes are fixed and reproducible. The assignment is completed before any purchase, meaning the distribution is determined independently of buyer behavior.

The Pachiverse Reveal is currently scheduled for Q3 2026. The reveal system finalisation is listed as in-progress in our public roadmap.

Ecosystem documentation and reveal system details will be released ahead of the reveal event through official project updates.

Pachiverse includes several NFT categories: Machine NFTs (the primary generative collection — pachinko/pachislot machines), Companion NFTs (planned for Q4 2026), and Mystery Packs (used in the member platform purchasing flow).

Additional asset categories and their ecosystem roles will be detailed in the forthcoming documentation releases.

Pachiverse NFTs are available on OpenSea under the PachiverseFoundation collection. OpenSea collection expansion is planned for Q3 2026.

NFT transfer functionality is already operational within the member platform inventory system.

Ecosystem Assets PV Coin, PVC, utility tokens 02 questions

PV Coin is a free member-distributed utility point used within the Pachiverse member platform. It is distributed to members as part of normal platform participation and is used for internal platform interactions. It is not a publicly traded token, investment product, or financial instrument.

PVC is the planned official ecosystem utility for Pachiverse. Its utility, issuance structure, platform role, and availability will be introduced only after formal documentation and ecosystem specifications are finalized. PVC is not currently available.
