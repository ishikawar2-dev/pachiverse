About

Where Pachinko Meets Web3

Pachiverse is a Web3 gaming universe built on the heritage of Japanese pachinko culture. Every machine, every spin, and every reward is tokenised on-chain — owned by you, tradeable by you, and governed by the community.

Through a fractional NFT model, players can hold stakes in real pachinko units, earn PV Coins through gameplay, and redeem mystery packs for new on-chain assets. Ownership is transparent, transferable, and always verifiable.

We are building the first on-chain entertainment ecosystem where the culture of play and the power of decentralised ownership converge into one seamless universe.

🎰

Own Your Gaming Assets

Pachinko machine NFTs exist on-chain as ERC-1155 tokens. Buy, sell, and transfer your units freely — no platform lock-in, no middleman.

⚡

Play & Earn

Log in daily, spin the gacha, and accumulate PV Coins across four rarity tiers. Every interaction with the ecosystem generates verifiable value.

🔷

Fractional Ownership

Hold as little as 1/200 of a pachinko unit. Collect fractional shares over time, merge them, and claim your full NFT Mystery Pack.
