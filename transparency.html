Transparency · phased disclosure

Transparency Notice

Pachiverse operates under phased disclosure. This page documents the current state of ecosystem transparency, what has been published, and what will be progressively disclosed as development advances.

Phased Development How disclosure is sequenced
Progressive disclosure
Pachiverse is currently under phased development. Smart contract addresses, metadata references, technical documentation, and ecosystem utility specifications will be progressively disclosed through official project updates and documentation releases.
Verification-first publication
Disclosures are sequenced to align with development milestones, deployment verification, and ecosystem readiness. Details are not published before they can be verified and properly documented.

Metadata & IPFS Asset metadata structure
IPFS-based metadata
NFT metadata is structured according to IPFS-based metadata standards. Metadata references and IPFS content identifiers (CIDs) will be disclosed alongside contract address publication.
Integrity guarantees
Metadata integrity is maintained through fixed-supply verification and ERC-1155 quantity management controls. Metadata will not be modified after the reveal system is finalized.
